# Left upper-arm/shoulder assembly instructions

- Preparation: 10 min
- Assembly: 15-20 min

## Requirement:
![](../img/left_upper_arm_shoulder_assembly_BOM.jpg)

**Subassembly**:
- Left upper arm *(displayed arm on picture)*

**3D Printed parts**:
- arm connector *(displayed shoulder on picture)*

**Actuators:**
- 1x Robotis Dynamixel MX28-AT (or MX28-T)

**Cables**:
- 1x 3P 200mm


**Robotis parts:**
- 1x HN07-N101
- 1x HN07-I101
- 8x Nuts M2.5
- 8x Bolts M2.5x6
- 6x Bolts M2x3

**Motor configuration:**
- 1x Alimentation 12V
- 1x SMPS2Dynamixel
- 1x USB2Dynamixel or USB2AX
- A computer...
